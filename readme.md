# The `docs/` Directory

Herein, the reader shall find accompanying notes concerning our generic [Data Science Project Template](https://github.com/kvn-dtrx/ds-project-template). More precisely, he shall find:

- [`dos-and-donts.md](./dos-and-donts.md): General recommendations and cautions for *Take Me Home* challenges.
- [`downgrading-options.md](./downgrading-options.md): A list of options for downgrading the template where necessary.
- [`final-checklist.md`](./final-checklist.md): A final checklist to be reviewed before the submission of a *Take Me Home* challenge.
- [`best-practices.md](./best-practices.md): Best practices for refining *Take Me Home* challenges.

*Mutatis mutandis*, the given advice is likewise applicable to data science projects more general than *Take Me Home* challenges.
